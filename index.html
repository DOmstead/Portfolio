About Me

"Hi, my name is David and I am a Full Stack Developer located in Phoenix, AZ. We could be responsible for the next great advancement in technology, or just make what we have even better. Let's build something together!"

Tech in nature, and the nature of tech, are two things I have always found fascinating. Right now researchers around the world are discovering that frogs are dying at an alarming rate, with many species potentially facing the threat of extinction. It's basically the Frogpocalypse.

Emerging tech could change that reality however, alongside changing reality as we understand it. Every year new advancements in technology change our relationship with the earth and have the potential to radically alter our understanding of it.

Seemingly unrelated tech can advance the human experience and change how we interact with the world. I have not yet seen a rainforest in person, but with an app on my phone I can. With VR I can walk through the trees. Improvements in AI and our evolving understanding of how to develop more capable neural networks is paving the way for rampant growth in many sectors, ranging from robotics to healthcare.

Even helping to improve the back-end logic and efficiency for an insurance provider could decrease nationwide premiums by 5%, and that could fund research that leads to a cure for humanities most devastating diseases.
